What can I do with NLTK?¶

A brief introduction to what you can (and can't) do with NLTK

from IPython.display import Image

Things that are pretty difficult to do in natural language processing:

  1. Understand complex emotions/sarcasm
  2. Go from written text to a series of facts
  3. Machine Translation
  4. Basically, understand text the way humans can understand text.

I would group the main things NLTK can do into four categories:

  1. Tokenising and counting those tokens
  2. Part of speech tagging
  3. Chunking and information extraction
  4. Machine learning with text
